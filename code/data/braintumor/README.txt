Brain tumor image data in this archive were obtained obtained from the MICCAI
2012 Challenge on Multimodal Brain Tumor Segmentation
(http://www.imm.dtu.dk/projects/BRATS2012) organized by B. Menze, A. Jakab, S.
Bauer, M. Reyes, M. Prastawa, and K. Van Leemput. The challenge database
contains fully anonymized images from the following institutions: ETH Zurich,
University of Bern, University of Debrecen, and University of Utah.
